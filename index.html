About Evening

More than instruments — we deliver the soul of music

Since 2016, London's Finest Instrument Boutique

Evening Music was founded by a group of passionate musicians in the heart of London. We travel the globe — from Steinway's Hamburg factory to the violin workshops of Cremona, from Gibson's Nashville custom shop to the Yamaha atelier in Tokyo — to bring the world's finest instruments to you.

Every instrument is meticulously set up by our master technicians before it leaves our showroom. We believe a great instrument unlocks limitless creativity.
